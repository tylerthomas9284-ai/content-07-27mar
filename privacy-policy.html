Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you hold over your personal information.

Last Updated: 1 June 2025

Summary: Meridian Insights Ltd collects only the personal data necessary to operate our publication and communicate with our readers. We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with advertisers. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@meridianinsights.com.

1. Who We Are

Meridian Insights Ltd ("Meridian", "we", "us", "our") is registered in England and Wales (Company No. 11234567). Our registered address is 12 Canary Wharf Tower, London, E14 5AB, United Kingdom. We are the data controller for personal information collected through this website.

For privacy-related enquiries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@meridianinsights.com.

2. What Data We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you contact us, subscribe to our newsletter, or use our contact form, we may collect your name, email address, organisation name, and the content of your messages. We only collect information that is necessary for the specific purpose you are providing it.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, our servers automatically collect technical information including your IP address, browser type and version, the pages you visit, the time and duration of your visit, and the website that referred you to us. We use this information solely for site performance analysis and security purposes.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use essential cookies necessary for the operation of our website, and analytical cookies that help us understand how visitors use our site. Analytical cookies are only set with your consent. You can withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or contacting us. For full details, please see our Cookie Policy section below.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the personal data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To deliver our newsletter and editorial communications to subscribers
  • To respond to enquiries submitted through our contact form
  • To analyse website traffic and improve site performance
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our systems
  • To comply with our legal obligations

We do not use your personal data for automated decision-making or profiling. We do not use your personal data for advertising purposes, and we do not sell or share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: For newsletter subscriptions and analytical cookies
  • Legitimate interests: For responding to enquiries and maintaining site security
  • Legal obligation: Where processing is required to comply with law

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected. Newsletter subscriber data is retained for as long as your subscription is active. Contact form data is retained for up to 12 months following resolution of the enquiry. Website analytics data is retained in aggregated, anonymised form.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal data
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • The right to restrict or object to processing of your data
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@meridianinsights.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

7. Cookie Policy

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They do not store personally identifiable information and are set in response to actions you take, such as setting your privacy preferences.

Analytical Cookies

With your consent, we use analytical cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This data is collected in aggregate and is used solely to improve our content and user experience. You can withdraw consent at any time.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website. For guidance on managing cookies in common browsers, visit the browser's help documentation.

8. Third-Party Services

Our website may use the following third-party services, each operating under their own privacy policies: web hosting infrastructure providers, email delivery services for newsletter distribution, and website analytics tools. We select these providers carefully and ensure they provide appropriate data protection guarantees.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated to newsletter subscribers via email. Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
