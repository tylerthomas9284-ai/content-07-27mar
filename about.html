Our Story

Clarity in a World of Noise

Meridian Insights was founded in 2018 on a simple conviction: that the world's most consequential decisions are made better when the people making them have access to genuinely rigorous, genuinely independent analysis.

Who We Are

An Independent Voice for Serious Professionals

We are a team of analysts, journalists, and former practitioners with deep expertise across strategy, finance, technology, and operations. We do not accept advertising. We do not publish sponsored content. We have no commercial relationships with the companies or industries we cover.

Our independence is not an aspiration — it is a structural commitment, reflected in how we are owned, funded, and governed. Every article published under the Meridian name represents the unfiltered view of its author, subject only to our editorial standards of accuracy, fairness, and rigour.

What We Stand For

Our Editorial Principles

01
Independence

We accept no advertising, no sponsored content, and no commercial arrangements that could compromise our editorial judgement. Our readers' trust is our most valuable asset — and we treat it accordingly.

02
Rigour

Every claim we make is sourced, every analysis is verifiable, and every opinion is clearly labelled as such. We hold ourselves to the standards of the best long-form journalism and academic research.

03
Relevance

We write for professionals who make consequential decisions, not for general audiences seeking entertainment. Clarity, applicability, and genuine insight are our editorial standards — not volume or velocity.

04
Accuracy

We correct errors promptly and transparently. When the facts change, we update our analysis. We do not allow published articles to become outdated without clear disclosure.

05
Fairness

We represent the full range of credible perspectives on complex issues, clearly distinguishing between matters of fact and matters of judgement. We do not misrepresent the views of those we disagree with.

06
Expertise

Our contributors bring practitioner experience alongside analytical capability. We do not publish generalist commentary on specialist topics — every piece is reviewed by someone with domain-level expertise.
